Quickbooks - Create Invoice In-App Walkthrough

This skill shows the steps to be followed to create an invoice in QuickBooks. This skill requires a valid QuickBooks account and works best with 'Business View'. So make sure to switch to 'Business View' on the application before triggering the skill.

QuickBooks - Receive Payments In-App Walkthrough
